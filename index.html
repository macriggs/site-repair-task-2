Reasons to shop with us

We are a Australian online pet store selling products for dogs, cats, fish, birds, small animals and reptiles. We offer high quality pet supplies and products at a cheap price online. Although we are Australian based pet shop, we ship our products to Australia, USA, Canada, United Kingdom and other countries.

The pet supplies we display on our website are of the highest quality. The products we sell and recommend at our pet shop are legitimate and trusted by us to be used on our own animals as well as our customers. We have owned a pet shop and been involved in the pet industry since 2003. We made the jump to the internet and started our online pet store in 2008.

If there is a product you are after but it is not on our website please Contact Us and we will try our best to get it in stock for you.
